The Best Movies According to Saheli

My top 3 movies of all-time.

A Silent Voice

This is my favourite anime.A young man is ostracized by his classmates after he bullies a deaf girl to the point where she moves away. Years later, he sets off on a path for redemption.

Taare Zameen Par

This movie has a beautiful message.It is about daydreamer Ishaan who is sent to boarding school,where an unconventional art teacher tries to help the imaginative student discover his true identity.

The Pursuit of Happyness

This movie portrays a very important message of life where a newly single father determined to lift himself and his son out of poverty works his way up from the bottom at a stock brokerage firm.
